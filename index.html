Should I use a resume template or create my own?

We highly recommend you use a resume template created by professionals, as they’re designed to pass the applicant tracking system (ATS) most employers use to filter out applicants. It will also save you the trouble of figuring out the correct formatting, font sizes and font colors.

At MyPerfectResume, we have dozens of great resume templates available you can use to build a resume online, including templates for creative and modern jobs. You can also download one of our free resume templates, but if you want to save time and use our step-by-step guidance, our Resume Builder is always a safe option.

How long should a resume be?

The length of your resume will depend on your level of experience. A professional resume should be one to two pages long. If you’re a student, a recent graduate or an entry-level professional, it’s OK for your resume to only be one page. But if you have several years of relevant experience, you can organize your qualifications into two pages.

Whatever your case may be, our free Resume Builder allows you to make your resume as long as necessary without compromising readability.

How far back should a resume go?

Our general advice is to outline your last 10 years of work experience in your professional resume unless you’re applying for a job that requires even more experience. Employers usually prefer resumes that are two pages long, at most.
